Mehran Karimzadeh's GitHub Page

Mehran is currently a CIHR post-doctoral fellow co-supervised by Dr. Hani Goodarzi at UCSF and Dr. Bo Wang at University Health Network. He is interested in understanding the role of the upstream regulators of transcription, transcription factors and RNA binding proteins, in disease. Mehran is interested in developing deep learning models for inferring the activity of these proteins from single-cell multi-modal genomic data.
